  1. Let \(A\) be a \(2\times 2\) matrix and let \(\lambda_1, \lambda_2\) be eigenvalues of \(A\). Show that:

    1. \(\lambda_1 + \lambda_2 =\text{trace}(A)\)
    2. \(\lambda_1 \cdot \lambda_2 =\text{det}(A)\)
